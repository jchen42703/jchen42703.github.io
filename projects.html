Projects and Research

Research

Capsule Networks for the Automated Segmentation of Left Atrium in Cardiac MRI

  • Dataset: Medical Segmentation Decathlon
  • Technologies Used: (Python 3.5) Keras, Tensorflow, Nibabel, Numpy, batchgenerators
  • Compared capsule networks to convolutional neural networks for the automated segmentation of left atrium in mono-modal MRI. One of the first cases of applying capsule networks for image segmentation and proposes a new capsule network architecture that performs better than the tested capsule network architectures (2018-2019).
  • Awards: New York City Science and Engineering Fair Finalist, NYC Junior Science and Humanities Symposium Finals in Computer Science

Convolutional Neural Networks for the Automated Segmentation of Soft-Tissue Sarcoma

  • Dataset: Segmenting Soft Tissue Sarcomas (Kaggle Dataset)
  • Technologies Used: (Python 3.5) Keras, Tensorflow, Numpy
  • Applied convolutional neural networks for the automated segmentation of soft-tissue sarcoma. First case of this in literature (2017).
  • Awards: New York City Science and Engineering Fair Finalist (2nd in Computer Science), Con Edison Money Award ($500)
